how did i get here?

I began my career as a mechanical engineer, working for ten years designing cables in CAD. During that time, I enrolled in night classes at UCLA Extension and discovered a passion for visual design. Naturally, after working in design for a few years, I realized that technology is my strength. I'm hoping this experience at Thinkful will help me bridge the gap between my tech skills and my desire to create.
